About MWPUAE Platform

Who We Are

METAL WOOD PLASTIC TECHNOLOGY FZC LLC (MWPUAE) is a licensed and approved B2B platform headquartered in the United Arab Emirates. We are officially registered under Trade Licence Number 262281621888 and Corporate Tax Registration Number 105015084400001, ensuring full compliance with UAE regulations.

Our platform, www.mwpuae.ae, is designed to connect genuine buyers and verified suppliers in the fields of Metal, Wood, Plastic, Machinery, and Technology, with the flexibility to handle other categories on special request.
